Kentucky Department of Education Commissioner's Student Advisory Council

In May 2023, I was selected to be a member of the Kentucky Department of Education Commissioner's Student Advisory Council for the 2023-2024 school year. As a member, I attend both in-person meetings at Frankfort, Kentucky, and virtual meetings where we discuss issues of critical importance to our education systems. Such topics include diversity, equity, inclusion, mental health awareness, and other key issues. In sharing our unique perspectives, we help advise the KDE and the Education Commissioner on ways to tackle and help solve issues that we find to be of most critical importance.
