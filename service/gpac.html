GO Pantry Ambassador Council

After being involved with local food pantry work via Action Ministries as well as global food insecurity research for the Purdue Youth Institute, I came across an opportunity to further explore this service route as a member of the GO Pantry Ambassador Council. As Ambassadors, we help brainstorm ideas and plan events, such as food drives with local businesses and schools, to help reduce food insecurity in the Northern Kentucky region. We also come up with ways to further support the GO Pantry mission, whether it be through official GO Pantry initiatives or our own initiatives.
