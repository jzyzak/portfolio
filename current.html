What Am I Up To?

Currently, I am working on two main research projects. The first project is with Dr. Jaeger at the University of Louisville, and we are currently utilizing Python in an attempt to describe and potentially simulate colloidal self-assembly. The second research project is with Dr. Liang at Texas A&M in an attempt to optimize the parameters of the existing MedCLIP medical image classification model. I have also submitted my project regarding the Development of a Novel Aroma Capture Device to the Regeneron Science Talent Search (RSTS), Junior Science and Humanities Symposium, and the Davidson Fellows Scholarship. Furthermore, I have recently started learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and will continue to master these languages as well as various others for future projects.
