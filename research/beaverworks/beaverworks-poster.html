MIT Beaver Works Summer Institute (BWSI) Autonomous Underwater Vehicle Challenge Poster

Over the course of about a month from July to August 2023, I was part of a group dedicated to designing and programming our autonomous underwater vehicles to perform a variety of functions. These included making it perform choreography, simulate a hunter algorithm, and simulate a prey algorithm. For our group, we were awarded the Red October Strategists award at the end of the program for most closely following the challenge in terms of our algorithms.
