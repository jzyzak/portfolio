Improving Plant-Based Hamburger Flavor Through Studying Flavor Formation in Grilled Beef Hamburger

We submitted our plant-based work regarding our study of flavor formation in beef- vs. plant-based burgers to the Fall 2022 National American Chemical Society (ACS) conference in Chicago. We had successfully identified 2-acetyl-2-thiazoline (2-AT) as an important aroma contributor for beef burgers that was almost completely absent in plant-based burgers. In further studying the formation of this compound, we hoped to identify the potential applications for formulating this compound in plant-based burgers to further replicate the flavor profile of a beef burger and make plant-based alternatives more appealing to consumers.
