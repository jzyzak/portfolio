Forensics/Speech & Debate

I have been a member of my school's Forensics/Speech and Debate team since freshman year and even served as debate captain (2021-2022 & 2023-2024). Since starting out with Impromptu and Congressional Debate, I have also expanded to Extemporaneous speaking. With these events, I have received many top placements, including State Semifinalist for Extemp (2023 and 2024), State Quarterfinalist for Impromptu (2024), State Champion for Congress (2024), and National Qualifier for the National Speech and Debate Tournament in Congress (2023) and International Extemp (2024).
